WHAT IS THE SÄRVÄNTHÄ?

I created SÄRVÄNTHÄ as a fictional multiverse for my upcoming fantasy novel. Särvänthä means all-encompassing or everything. As a hobbyist author, many of my practice one-shots and stories happen in this multiverse. The upcoming series Tales of Shambhala will take place in one of the worlds of Särvänthä. I've been writing this particular outline for quite a time and recent trends in the LitRPG genre inspired me to add it as one of the many worlds of Särvänthä. The planned release of the book A Tale of Bonds is somewhere around the fourth Quater of 2020. Though snippets of world-building and lore will be posted on this site.

While you wait for the book to release, please enjoy the illustartions below.

Or if you want a glimpse inside the world of Krastos head over to Royal Road. Where King Amaranthine has woken up from deep slumber.
