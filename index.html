WHAT IS THE SÄRVÄNTHÄ?

I created SÄRVÄNTHÄ as a fictional multiverse for my upcoming fantasy novel. Särvänthä means all-encompassing or everything. As a hobbyist author, many of my practice one-shots and stories happen in this multiverse. The upcoming series Tales of Shambhala and Amaranthine Chronicles take place in world of Kraastos but in different time periods. I've been writing these two outline for quite a time and recent trends in the LitRPG genre inspired me to add it as one of the many worlds of Särvänthä. The planned release of the book A Tale of Bonds is on fourth Quater of 2020 is debatble due to personal work. On the talk of Amaranthine Chronicles the first draft is ready and going through some heavy editing. Though snippets of world-building and lore which is common to both worlds will be posted on this site.
