WHAT IS THE SÄRVÄNTHÄ?

I created SÄRVÄNTHÄ as a fictional multiverse for my upcmoing LitRPG fantasy novel. Särvänthä means all encompasing or everything. As a hobbist author, many of my practise oneshots and stories happen in this multiverse. The upcoming series Tales of Shambhala will take place in one of the worlds of Särvänthä. I've been writing this particular outline for quite a time and recent trend in LitRPG inspired me to add it as one of the many worlds of Särvänthä. The planned release of book A Tale of Bonds is planned somewhere around fourth Quater of 2020. Though snippets of world buidling and lore will be posted on this site.
